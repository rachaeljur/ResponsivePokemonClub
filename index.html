Welcome to the Pokemon Fan Club

Join us every Tuesday and Thursday at Cafe Passion for Pokemon!

About Us: Our Pokemon Fan Club is a group of Pokemon lovers who meet every Tuesday and Thursday to play Pokemon, trade cards and socialise within a like minded community. We meet at a cafe owned by Mr.Peter Pika Saur and his wife Alice fondly known as Mrs Pika Saur. His name given inspired by his parents love for Pikachu and Bulbasaur and Japanese heritage. For years they have met lots of Pokemon lovers over the years. The Pika Saurs continue this tradition. The owners run a normal successful cafe during the day where Pokemon is encouraged and specifically on Tuesdays and Thursdays open the cafe for club purposes - 5-9pm. Please read on for further details.
Our Clubs: Our clubs run every Tuesday and Thursday at Cafe Passion for Pokemon. The fun sessions start at 5-8pm where our avid club members trade cards, discuss Pokemon, play pokemon cardgames and often simple foods and drinks for example, Pikachu style cupcakes, Charmander Gingerbread, Bulbasaur biscuits and much more. The club is really fun and many attend creating a warm, friendly community spirit. Often friendships and bonds are made and other community events occur which, is really important in an isolating city like London.
